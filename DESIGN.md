# Design Context

Color strategy: restrained product palette with tinted neutral surfaces and one teal accent for actions, focus, and selected states.

Theme scene: a reviewer uses the app on a laptop in a bright interview setting, scanning tables and evidence quickly.

Typography: Geist/system sans for UI, Geist Mono for IDs, counts, dates, and monetary values.

Components: top navigation, dense tables, compact filters, KPI tiles, bar-list charts, evidence panels, and clear empty/error states.

Accessibility: keyboard focus rings, semantic headings, form labels, visible AI-disabled state, no text-only icon buttons without labels.
