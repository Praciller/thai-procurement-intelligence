# Product Context

Register: product

Product purpose: Thai Public Procurement Intelligence is a personal portfolio data product for searching, analyzing, and summarizing Thai public procurement open data.

Users: AI Engineer and Data Engineer interviewers, developers exploring procurement data, and analysts who want a working demo.

Primary workflow: ingest sample or public CSV data, search and filter records, inspect a record, generate or view an AI summary, and ask evidence-based questions.

Tone: professional, precise, public-sector data product. No flashy campaign language.

Anti-references: no low-code dashboard feel, no generic SaaS hero, no fabricated real-agency claims, no private or employer data.
